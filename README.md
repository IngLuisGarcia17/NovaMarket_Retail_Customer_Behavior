# NovaMarket_Retail_Customer_Behavior

**NovaRetail+** is a major e-commerce platform in Latin America, serving millions of users. As the **2024 fiscal year-end** approaches, the **Growth and Retention team** is conducting a strategic analysis to address a critical business question:

> **"Which customer behavior factors are most strongly associated with the total annual revenue generated?"**

This project focuses on uncovering the key drivers of profitability and identifying patterns that can improve long-term customer retention.
The `NovaMarket` dataset includes 15,000 customer records.
## 📂 Repository Content

- `notebooks/Project_NovaRetail_customer_behavior.ipynb`
  → The primary notebook featuring:
    * **Statistical Correlation:** Integrating Pearson, Spearman, and Phik methods to identify linear and non-linear relationships.
    * **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Identifying significant relationships through advanced visualizations, including **Scatterplots** and **Heatmaps**.
    * **Insight Validation:** Detecting and filtering misleading or spurious correlations to ensure data reliability.
    * **Strategic Documentation:** Defining and documenting assumptions and technical limitations.
    * **Business Translation:** Converting complex technical findings into **actionable business recommendations**.
    * **Executive Reporting:** Drafting a professional, high-impact technical report for decision-makers.
